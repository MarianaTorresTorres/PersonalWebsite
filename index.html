Projects

MEMBERSHPEUF.COM

Senior Software Engineering Director

Membershpe UF is the official website for the UF chapter of the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers. Our software engineering team created this website to facilitate the management of the point system that our chapter works with. Some additional features are a corporate database, rentals of engineering equipment (like Arduinos), a SHPEito network for members to find other people in their classes/major/year, and a variety of administrative tools for the Executive Board to manage SHPE. All of this was accomplished using the MERNG stack: MongoDB, Express, React, NodeJS, and GraphQL.

SHPE UF MOBILE APP

Scrum Master

This is the mobile application that will complement the SHPE UF website. Unlike the website, this app will be user-oriented and provide SHPE UF members greater accesibility to their points, events, and tasks. This project uses the MERNG stack as well, but with React Native. To be launched in September 2021.

SOLSTICE PLAZA

Developer

Solstice Plaza is a Virtual Reality platform especifically designed for students of the University of Florida. Using Maya, EchoAR, and Unity, my team and I were able to create a VR version of UF'S Plaza of the Americas and Library West. Students can join the server and explore the 3D environment, as well as interact with other students virtually. Since the pandemic has taken away the opportunity of living on campus for many students, Solstice Plaza is our way of making that experience accesible once again. Winner of the Hack Solstice 2021 Best Use of EchoAR Category.
